Nature and Calm Design

Web designers often use natural landscapes and cool color palettes to create relaxing interfaces. Blues, greens and soft gradients are known to reduce visual stress.

Background Images

CSS allows an image to live behind your content like a wallpaper. This image can stay fixed, repeat like tiles, or stretch to fill the screen.
